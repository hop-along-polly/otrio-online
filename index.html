Otrio

Player 1's Turn

Welcome to Otrio

A strategic ring-placement game for 2–4 players. The board is a 3×3 grid. Each cell has a large ring, a medium ring, and a small peg.

Your Pieces

Each player has 9 pieces: 3 large rings, 3 medium rings, and 3 small pegs. Your pieces are shown in your player bank around the board.

Placing Pieces

Drag a piece from your player bank and drop it onto a matching empty slot on the board. Large on large, medium on medium, peg on peg.

Win: Same Size

Get three pieces of the same size in a row, column, or diagonal. Here, three medium green rings form a winning line.

Win: Ordered Size

Place three pieces in ascending or descending size order along a row, column, or diagonal. Small → Medium → Large (or reversed).

Win: Concentric

Stack all three sizes — large ring, medium ring, and small peg — of your color in the same single cell. This is the trickiest win to spot!

Tips

Watch all three win conditions at once. Block your opponents while building your own lines. Use Undo (or Ctrl+Z) to take back a move. Have fun!
